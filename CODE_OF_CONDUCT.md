# Code of Conduct

ScreenCopy should be a practical, respectful project for users and contributors.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope and tradeoffs.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith disruption.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions and restrict participation when needed to keep the project usable and safe.
